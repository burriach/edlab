**Young Academy of Spain**

<!--more-->
Marcos Fernández-Martínez has been accepted into the Young Academy of Spain, a group that brings together outstanding early-career researchers to promote scientific excellence, interdisciplinary collaboration, and public engagement. The academy supports talented young scientists and gives them a platform to contribute to research and society. The cerimony took place in València, in November 2025.
