**Spanish National Research Prize 2025**

<!--more-->
The cerimony took place in Pedralbes Palace, in Barcelona, in May 2026. Marcos has been recognised for his outstanding scientific career in the study of the carbon cycle and biodiversity, with pioneering contributions that integrate ecology, biogeochemistry and the analysis of complex systems. His leadership in international projects and his publications have had a significant international impact, establishing him as an emerging leading figure in global ecology.
